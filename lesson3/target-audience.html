Target Audience

Our target audience for this website is both business owners, and consumers looking for local businesses to support. Ideally, startups and established businesses looking for an ethical, honest, and supportive locale to build in would be so enticed by our website, they would connect with us. Consumers, local and visiting, would be able to visit the West Jordan Chamber of Commerce for local businesses, highlights, local political matters, and general information. This double pronged approached mirrors our values of being open, accessible, and informative.

Persona One

Hugh Mungous owns small gyms in other municipalities and has contacted the West Jordan Chamber of Commerce to see if his business model could be profitable in our region. A successful entrepreneur would be an amazing resource for the Chamber of Commerce. Mentoring other small business owners, leadership, and a voice of experience is always welcome.

Persona Two

Tiana Bo Bana is an artist that is currently looking to move to our city. She runs an online startup that is really taking off. She has asked if there are any other businesses that work in the art industry in our membership. She is not looking to take market share from other local artists. Since her online business services primarily international and non-local clients, the Chamber is not worried about that. In fact, our local print and framing shop needs her business! She is looking for a caring community that work together more than compete. West Jordan is the place for her and her business.
